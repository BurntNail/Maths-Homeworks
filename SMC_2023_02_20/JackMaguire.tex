\( C \) points form a circle around \(A\), radius 2. You need to find the unit vector of \(A\), then scale it by 2. Then, either add/subtract that from \(A\) to get the max/min.

\begin{align*}
	|OA| &= \sqrt{1^2 + 2^2} = \sqrt{5} \\
	OA_\text{Unit} &= \frac{OA}{\sqrt{5}} \\
	&= \frac{1}{\sqrt{5}}i - \frac{2}{\sqrt{5}}j \\
	&= \frac{\sqrt{5}}{5}i - \frac{2\sqrt{5}}{5}j \\
	|AC| &= \frac{2\sqrt{5}}{5}i - \frac{4\sqrt{5}}{5}j \\ \\
	\text{Max} &= i - 2j + \frac{2\sqrt{5}}{5}i - \frac{4\sqrt{5}}{5}j \\
	\text{Min} &= i - 2j - \frac{2\sqrt{5}}{5}i + \frac{4\sqrt{5}}{5}j
\end{align*}
